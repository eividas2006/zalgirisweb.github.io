History of Zalgiris
Zalgiris was founded in 1944. Zalgirs has been licenced in the Euroleague since 2011 and it still in the league today! Zalgiris plays in the Betsson LKL, it's a Lithuanian league, and they have 25 titles being the best team in Lithuania, as Zalgiris has a rival team in LKL, Vilnius Rytas, that is the 2nd best team in Lithuania with 7 titles to their name. Zalgiris competes in the KMT Championship, its a external league in lithunia. Zalgiris Kaunas has one euroleague title, they won it in the 1998-1999 season. There were a lot of different proffessional players that have played in Zalgiris Kaunas. 2 legends for the club's history and records, Arvydas Sabonis and Sarunas Jasikevicius, two Lithuanians who dominated basketball in the 90s to 2000s. Arvydas then went on to play to the Nba and retired and came back to live in Lithuania, and who is now a business man who owns a restaurant in Palanga with his wife and is a public figure and influence to the Lithuanian fans. Sarunas Jasikevicius was also one of the legends that played in lithuania for Zalgiris and left a rememberance place for Lithuanians to look back at. He has played in the Nba for big teams, golden state and pacers. Sarunas Retired in 2014 while playing in Zalgiris and he then started his coaching career for zalgiris for 2 season and he was named best player and coach in Zalgiris, he coached Barcelona from 2020 to 2023 and now he coaches in Fenerbache. He won the Euroleague title during the 2024-2025 season with fenerbache, he was named the best coach of the year.


Where We Are Based
Zalgiris Kaunas BC is Based in Kaunas in the Baltics in Lithuania. The Arena that the Zalgiris games are held at are Zalgirio Arena, with a capacity of roughly 15'000 for basketball games, and around 20'000 or more for any other events, like concerts, ice skating games or boxing matches. The fans in Zalgirio Arena are the most passionate and hard on felt fans in Lithuania, because the fans will alweays be there whent the team wins and loses, that is what seperates other fans from the rest, they will all stay to the end and do a viking routine clapping chant for the players to say to them as a thank you for always being there for them.
